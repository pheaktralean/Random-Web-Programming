Welcome To Strawberri Paws Events

Welcome to Strawberri Paws Events! We are thrilled to invite you and your beloved pets to join our exciting and heartwarming gatherings. Our events are designed to create joyous moments and lasting memories for you and your furry friends. From our Monthly Summer Picnic to the weekly swimming classes, every event is a chance for your pets to socialize, play, and have fun. We also have special adoption events and themed parties, like our Party Animals celebration, where your pets can meet new friends and find their forever homes.

About Us

Strawberri Paws Adoption is dedicated to finding loving homes for cats and dogs in need. Our mission is to match pets with the perfect families and ensure they have a happy life together.
