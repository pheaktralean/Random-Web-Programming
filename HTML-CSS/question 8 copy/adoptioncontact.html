Contact Us

Congratulations on thinking about adopting a dog or cat! We are thrilled to assist you in finding your new furry friend. If you are considering giving away your dog or cat, we understand this can be a difficult decision, and we are here to help ensure your pet finds a loving new home. We are always happy to help you find the perfect pet to adopt or to find a new home for your beloved pet. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us. Our team is here to support you every step of the way.

About Us

Strawberri Paws Adoption is dedicated to finding loving homes for cats and dogs in need. Our mission is to match pets with the perfect families and ensure they have a happy life together.
